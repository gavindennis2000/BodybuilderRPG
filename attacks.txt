The fight menu will have four actions:
  attack - abilities to hurt enemies and buff stats
  items - use groceries from G&C Supplements
  run - flee from confrontations
  max out - overdrive/limit break type attack

attacks
  Hitters
    Dumbbell Press - Low damage physical attack
    Barbell Press - Med damage physical attack
    Hammer Strength Press - High damage physical attack
  
  Buffs
    Wrist wrap - Increases power
    Knee wrap - Increases toughness
    
  "Spells"
    leg press - jumps high into the sky, fleeing battles and teleporting from city to city when on the overworld
